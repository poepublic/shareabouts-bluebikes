{{#_}}Site Notes:{{/_}}

  • {{#_}}Suggestions do not guarantee a station; rather, suggestions are helpful feedback to augment existing processes that determine where and when new stations are added or expanded.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Most of our stations are 42–52’ long and 6’ wide.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}For durability and easy maintenance, we prefer level hardscape (concrete, brick, or asphalt) in public spaces with at least 4 hours of direct sunlight daily. If hardscape is unavailable, our team may be able to install concrete pads.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Stations can also be placed on low-traffic, low-speed streets, ideally next to bike lanes.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Please identify any utility covers or maintenance holes, as stations cannot be installed over them.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Sites on federal, state, or private property require approval from the government or property owner and may need additional permissions{{/_}}
